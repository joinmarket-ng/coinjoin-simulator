JoinMarket maker clustering & taker anonymity-set reduction (May 2026)

A passive on-chain adversary clusters JoinMarket maker wallets through protocol-mandated mixdepth-rotating change outputs, at precision = 1.0 by construction. On the full mainnet corpus (23,876 JM CoinJoins, 16,890 ILP-decoded), the v6 clusterer recovers 74,471 certified wallet components. Each certified maker shrinks the taker's per-CJ anonymity set.
